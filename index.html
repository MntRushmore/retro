What is a retro game?

A retro game is a video game from or inspired by the golden age of gaming—think the '80s and '90s. With pixel art, chiptune music, and nostalgic vibes, these games feel like time machines made of pixels and neon.

How do you make a retro game?

Making a retro game is like building a digital time machine. Start with a simple idea—like a pixel hero or a dodgy spaceship. Add pixel art, sprinkle in chiptune beats, and make sure the controls feel tight. Keep the gameplay fun, fast, and nostalgic. Wrap it all in a vintage title screen—and boom, you’ve got your own 8-bit masterpiece. Need ideas? Check out the Examples page.
